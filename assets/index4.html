WHO WE ARE?

WVDI TEAM

Our team at Western Visayas Driving Institute Corp. is proud to provide high-quality driving education, thanks to their hard work and dedication. Our success is also a testament to the trust and confidence placed in us by our students. Join us on this journey towards shaping safe and confident drivers.

WESTERN VISAYAS DRIVING INSTITUTE CORP.

At Western Visayas Driving Institute Corp., we’re more than just a driving school. We’re trailblazers in the industry, leading the way for safe and affordable driving education for over 20 years. Our commitment to quality is unmatched, as evidenced by our accreditation from the LTO and our comprehensive packages that go beyond just driving lessons.

 

We believe that driving is a lifelong skill, which is why we offer free class lectures on important topics like defensive driving, preventive maintenance, site lectures, and even hands-on car maintenance. Our goal is to empower drivers with the knowledge and skills they need to hit the road with confidence and safety.

 

Join us on our mission to make the roads a safer place for everyone. Sign up for our affordable, high-quality driving lessons today!

Student's Permit

Student

For Filipinos


Qaulifications:
  1. Must be at least seventeen (17) years old
  2. Must be physically and mentally fit to operate a motor vehicle
  3. Must be able to read and write in Filipino or English
Requirements
  1. Properly Accomplished Application for Driver’s License
  2. PSA Authenticated Birth Certificate with Official Receipt (original and photocopy)
  3. If Married, PSA Authenticated Marriage Contract (original and photocopy)
  4. Medical Certificate issued by LTO Accredited Physician
  5. If below eighteen (18) years of age, original copy of parental/guardian’s consent with original and photocopy of one (1) valid government issued Identification Card containing the signature of the consenting parent/guardian;

For Foreigners


Qaulifications:
  1. Must be eighteen (18) years old
  2. Must know how to read and write in English or Filipino
  3. Must be physically and mentally fit to operate a motor vehicle
  4. Must have arrived in the Philippines for at least one (1) month prior to the date of application with LTO and can show evidence from BID/DFA that he will stay in the country for at least 12 months (1 year) from the date of application
Requirements
  1. Properly Accomplished Application for Driver’s License
  2. Birth Certificate (original and photocopy)
  3. Medical Certificate issued by LTO Accredited Physician
  4. Passport (original and photocopy)
  5. Alien Certificate of Registration I-card
  6. Original and photocopy of passport with entry date of at least one (1) month and visa duration of at least one (1) year from date of application, or if born in the Philippines, present original and photocopy of birth certificate duly authenticated by the NSO.
